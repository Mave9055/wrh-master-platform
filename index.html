Company Overview

Capitol Contracts LLC specializes in comprehensive facility management, including maintenance, landscaping, janitorial services, and subcontracted HVAC and plumbing services. With a focus on government contracts, we ensure efficient project management and on-time delivery through trusted subcontractor coordination and professional oversight.

Founded by Daniel Lingar, an aspiring Project Manager and Service-Disabled Veteran, our company is built on the core values of integrity, precision, and dedication to the mission. We are committed to supporting federal and state agencies with high-quality, reliable facility solutions.
