Facilitator Core Rules
⏸
PAUSE
Count to 3 silently after any reframe or key line. Let it land before moving on.
🔉
DROP
Lower your voice on the second half of an important sentence. The drop signals weight.
🫁
BREATHE
Take one slow breath before starting the rapid reframes. Model regulation.
👁
EYES
Look at one person (not the whole room) when asking a mirror question. Create contact.
🚫
HARD LIMIT
No more than 2 sessions back-to-back. Period. Your nervous system matters too.
📝
DECOMPRESSION
After each session: write one thing you noticed. Shake out hands. Drink water. Leave the session there.

Facilitator Decompression Protocol
"After each session, take 5 minutes alone. Write down one thing you noticed about the room — not to fix, just to see. Then shake out your hands, drink water, and leave the session there. You are not responsible for their healing. You are responsible for the container."
Hard limit: No more than 2 sessions back-to-back. Schedule decompression time as a non-negotiable operational requirement, not a luxury.
